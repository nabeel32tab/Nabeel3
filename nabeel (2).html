Understanding Cybercrime

A Neon Tech Cybersecurity Experience

What is Cybercrime?

  • Illegal computer/network-based activity targeting personal and organizational data
  • Hacking, identity theft, online fraud, cyberbullying, ransomware
  • Regulated by laws like the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act

Types of Cybercrime

  • Phishing & Social Engineering
  • Malware & Ransomware Attacks
  • Data Breaches & Identity Theft
  • Cyberbullying & Harassment
  • Denial-of-Service Attacks & Botnets

Consequences

  • Financial losses & reputational damage
  • Legal repercussions
  • Psychological trauma for victims
  • National security risks

Prevention & Protection

  • Strong passwords & multi-factor authentication
  • Regular software updates and backups
  • User education & incident response plans
  • Advanced cybersecurity tools and frameworks
